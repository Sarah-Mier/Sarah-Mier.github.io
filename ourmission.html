A female-forward approach to career building

Have you heard... men apply for a job when they meet only 60% of the qualifications, but women apply only if they meet 100%?

Hey ladies, you miss 100% of the shots you don't take! We think you're qualified, you just need a little bit of help from women who have already outfoxed the job hunt. We believe you should never feel trapped at a job. We want to help you get un-stuck.

We're women who have switched jobs, switched industries, and made total 180's in our careers when everyone said it couldn't be done! And now, we want to make it easier for career launching women to connect directly and meaningfully to other women who have forged similar career paths.

It should be as easy as simply saying 'Yes!' to other women that want to help. We believe together we're better. We hope you join us!
