Just like you!

Everyone starts somewhere. Our foxes were just in your shoes.

We're a small (but mighty!) growing group of women who have conquered our career doubts and moved to the next rung of the ladder. We didn't do it alone. We got help along the way and now we want to make it easier for you to find the right resources.

Foxes come in all shapes and sizes, from all industries, backgrounds and geographies. We're introverts, extroverts, technical and creative. We can relate to your experience.
